Pawan Kalyan, he inspires youth to fight for their future and fought along with them.

Here's a timeline of Pawan Kalyan's life:

  • 1971 - Born in Bapatla, Andhra Pradesh, India
  • 1996 - Makes his film debut with "Akkada Ammayi Ikkada Abbayi"
  • 1998 - Gains recognition for his role in "Tholi Prema," which wins the National Film Award for Best Feature Film in Telugu
  • 2001 - Stars in the blockbuster "Kushi," solidifying his status as a leading actor in Telugu cinema
  • 2003 - Appears in the film "Johnny," which he also directed, showcasing his versatility
  • 2008 - Experiences a significant hit with "Jalsa," directed by Trivikram Srinivas
  • 2011 - Stars in the successful film "Gabbar Singh," a remake of the Bollywood film "Dabangg"
  • 2014 - Launches his political party, Jana Sena Party, and campaigns for various social and political issues
